So for this game we will be testing how well everyone knows Sujata. Here are the rules:

  1. There will be a series of questions about Sujata. For each question, Sujata gets to pick one person to answer the question. Choose carefully since you can only pick a person once!
  2. If you guess incorrectly, fear not, there will be a series of hints to help you along the way
  3. Once you get the question right, you get to open the corresponding "gift" (the gifts are numbered). You have a timer to get it unwrapped so no dilly dallying!
  4. After you open the gift, you will be presented with a task that must be completed before you can proceed to the next question. Since X-Mas is a family affair, you can elicit help from the audience with the tasks

HAVE FUN!
